Bloody Mary

You feel like having a drink earlier in the day, but don't wanna look like a degen? Look no further. Loki reccommends you have a Bloody Mary. Reactions may vary

Ingredients

  • Tomato juice (120ml)
  • Vodka (60ml)
  • The spice of life (Tabasco, to taste)
  • Worcestire sauce
  • Lime juice
  • Wasabi

Preparation

  1. Add all ingredients on a shaker.
  2. Fill with ice (at least half-way). Shake until chilled (about 15s).
  3. Double sieve it to a glass with ice.
  4. (optional) Add a garnish. Traditionally, a stalk of cellery, but do not be afraid to be creative: garnish with left-over pizza if it's all you have.
