Eggnog

It's that time of the year for family, and you don't have the patience? Make it legen...DAIRY with this simple eggnog recipe.

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Bourbon (60ml)
  • Syrup
  • Whole (fresh) egg
  • Cinammon
  • Muskat nut

Preparation

  1. Add the Bourbon, egg, syrup to a cocktail shaker.
  2. Dry shake them for about 15s, until the egg yolk has mixed with everything.
  3. To the mixed liquid add ice (at least half-way) and cinammon. Shake until chilled.
  4. Pour it on your fanciest ice-filled glass.
  5. Top it off with milk. I use the word milk loosely here. Anything milk-like will do. Fruity yogurt drinks, chocolate milk? Go for it it's the holidays.
  6. Decorate with some cinammon powder (and a stick if you're feeling extra fancy) and muskat nut(grated).
