Miso Noodle Soup

Love Ramen, but couldn't be bothered to do it from scratch? This simple recipe is for you

Ingredients

  • Dashi
  • Miso paste
  • Noodles

    • (optional)

  • (hardboiled) Egg
  • Green onions

Preparation

  1. Add the packet of Dashi to 3 cups of water in a pot. Bring to boil.
  2. Add the noodles and wait fro a few minutes.
  3. While waiting remove between half and a cup of broth from the cooking noodles.
  4. Mix 1tbsp of miso paste to the removed broth
  5. Add the broth cup back into the pot.
  6. When the noodles are ready, remove from heat and serve.

    7. (optional)

  7. Cut the hardboiled egg in half (obviously, so that you get 2 teardrop shapes, idjit).
  8. Cut the green onions in small pieces.
  9. Put the 2 half eggs in the plate and sprinkle the green onions.
